About Me:

Hello, I’m Divya, a Senior Data Engineer with over 12+ years of experience in data integration, analysis, and reporting. I specialize in architecting scalable cloud solutions and optimizing costs using a diverse suite of technologies—including Informatica PowerCenter/IDMC, Azure Data Factory, Oracle, Snowflake, Teradata, Azure, AWS, Tableau, and Power BI—to drive scalable, data-driven decision-making.

Throughout my career, I have led multiple migration and cloud modernization initiatives. For instance, I have successfully extracted data from Oracle and migrated it to Snowflake, while developing robust ETL processes with Informatica PowerCenter/IDMC, Azure Data Factory, and Python. These efforts have optimized data flows, improved lineage tracking, and boosted reporting efficiency by 40-50%.

In the FinOps space, I have crafted insightful Power BI reports to analyze cloud consumption costs, uncovering savings plan opportunities and M365 licensing cost optimizations.

My expertise in Azure DevOps and CI/CD methodologies has ensured the seamless automation and deployment of data pipelines, enhancing overall system performance and scalability.

I also bring extensive experience in data governance and quality, having implemented frameworks like Informatica CDGC and data harmonization practices to maintain the integrity of both structured and unstructured data.

My strong command of SQL and PL/SQL further supports large-scale analytics and robust business data modeling.

I work closely with stakeholders to translate complex business requirements into actionable insights. From creating comprehensive Business Requirement Documents (BRDs), RDMs, high and low-level Design documents, and change management plans, to engaging in sprint planning and collaborating with architects and cross-functional teams, I ensure that each project is aligned with strategic goals and delivered efficiently using agile methodologies and tools such as Jira and Azure DevOps.

I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex business challenges and look forward to driving innovation and excellence in every project I undertake.
